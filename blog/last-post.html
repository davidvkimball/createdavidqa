Last post (probably)

After almost exactly a year, I'm finally putting a pin in this site's development formally.

Thanks to everyone for your grace while I transitioned this site from david.qa to create.dvdv.kim. I realized I need to hold on to that root domain for other future purposes.

In the meantime, I hope you've found the various guides, resources, and layouts helpful. I'll continue to keep this website up for as long as I have the domain name, and likely beyond that in case it becomes davidqa.netlify.app.

If you'd like to keep following me on my web journey, I encourage you to join me on my personal website where I'll continue writing about more topics like this.

Thank you for your support!

I'll see you on the other side.
